NEW RULES
1) we draw names if your team shows up with 4 or 5 players (only for singles play).
2) 5 players - Triples play 1 team short, doubles play 1 team short
3) 4 players - Triples play 1 short on both teams, doubles can be played as singles or you can lose a match
4) spare players can only be used to make up a team being short of 6 players or to make up for a team being short of 1 woman minimum. If you have 6, 7 or 8 regular players you CAN NOT use a spare!
